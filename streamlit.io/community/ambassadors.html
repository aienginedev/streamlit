🎓Become a Student Ambassador

As a Streamlit Student Ambassador, you’ll have the opportunity to become a social media influencer, get exclusive access to Streamlit experts, training and swag, build a digital portfolio to advance your career, and strengthen your leadership skills through hackathons and peer mentorship.

Why become a Streamlit Student Ambassador?

  • Great resources

    Free hosting resources for your apps and more private apps for your educational projects.

  • Educational content

    Streamlit educational content such as tutorials, videos, and more

  • We sponsor you

    Streamlit as a sponsor of events on your campus and/or for your student organization (and even Streamlit-related guest speakers!).

  • Online community

    An online forum for students using Streamlit

  • Features

    An opportunity to be featured on our blog, in our newsletter, and on our social media channels (visited by hundreds of thousands of developers!)

  • Swag!

    Exclusive hackathon invitations, and support to host your own

How does it work?

  1. 1

    Join monthly meetings

    Connect with our community of students using Streamlit and learn about the latest and greatest Streamlit happenings.

  2. 2

    Actively participate in the Discord channel

    Chat with other program participants in the dedicated Discord channel and share Streamlit projects you’re currently working on

  3. 3

    Share your apps on social media

    Unleash your Streamlit creations on social media and don’t forget to tag us.

  4. 4

    Participate in Streamlit hackathons and (optionally) host your own hackathon

    Hack with the Streamlit team during our internal hackathons a few times a year. Optionally, host a hackathon in your community with the support of the Streamlit team.
